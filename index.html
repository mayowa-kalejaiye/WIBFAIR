ABOUT US

About WIBFAIR

From Her Hands To Your Home

WIBFair is organized by Ebiere Empere, the visionary CEO of Okads Seafood and founder of Women in Business Initiative. This innovative platform was born out of her passion to empower every woman, especially entrepreneurs, and provide a supportive ecosystem for their growth.

This year, with the help of the community she has built, they have been committed to giving back to the female community, reminding them that they "see them".

WIBFair is dedicated to:

  • Fostering connections and collaborations among women in business
  • Providing access to resources, mentorship, and funding opportunities
  • Celebrating women's achievements and successes
  • Creating a supportive community that inspires and motivates
