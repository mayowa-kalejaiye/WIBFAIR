ABOUT UNBROKEN

Empowering Women to Rise

She Rose * She Rebuilt * She Reigns

Unbroken is a platform that empowers women, especially single mums and mature single ladies, to rise above life's challenges, heal from their brokenness, rediscover their identity, and embrace wholeness.

Every October, our annual Unbroken Conference gathers women from all walks of life for an unforgettable experience of healing, empowerment, and transformation.

Our Mission:

The mission of Unbroken is to empower women—especially single mums and mature single ladies—to rise above life's challenges, rediscover their true identity, and live fulfilled lives. We are committed to creating safe spaces for healing, emotional restoration, and self-discovery, while providing practical resources, mentorship, and opportunities for personal and financial growth.

Unbroken is dedicated to:

  • Creating safe spaces for healing and emotional restoration
  • Providing practical resources and mentorship opportunities
  • Fostering personal and financial growth opportunities
  • Equipping women with strength, confidence, and resilience

CONVENER

Bunmi Alabi

Mental Health Counselor, Writer & Host of Unbroken

Bunmi Alabi is a compassionate Mental Health Counselor, Writer, and Host of Unbroken—an annual women's empowerment conference. She is the founder of Oasis Counseling, where she provides therapy and guidance for single mums, young women, and singles navigating their journey of self-discovery.

As the author of "Couples' Waiting Room," Bunmi shares hope and practical steps for couples in their waiting season. Her passion is to help women heal from emotional pain, embrace their identity, and live fulfilled lives with confidence and purpose.

GUEST SPEAKER

Omotunde Adebowale David

(LOLO 1)

Media Executive | Broadcaster | Content Creator

LOLO 1 is a seasoned media executive and one of Nigeria's most celebrated broadcasters. With over 25 years of experience across radio, television, and digital media, she has made an indelible mark as a pioneer female pidgin radio host and media strategist.

From hosting the iconic Oga Madam Office Show on Wazobia FM to leading Lasgidi FM as General Manager, LOLO 1 currently co-hosts JASIRI on News Central TV—a bold platform for African women's voices.
